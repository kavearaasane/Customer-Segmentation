<h id="#overview"><b>OVERVIEW</h></b>
<p id="overview">Customer segmentation is the process of dividing customers into distinct groups based on their behavior, characteristics, or preferences. This approach is useful for businesses to better understand and serve their customers, tailor marketing strategies, and improve customer satisfaction.</p>
